# VaultDex App Icon Concept

- Use a deep navy background with a subtle premium gradient.
- Centre an original gold vault shield mark with a simple card silhouette inside.
- Add a small yellow lightning sparkle to suggest discovery and rare pulls.
- Keep the shape rounded, friendly and readable at small iOS icon sizes.
- Use the VaultDex navy and gold palette for a collectible-card feel.
- Do not include official Pokemon assets, Poké Ball-style marks, character art, third-party logos or copyrighted card artwork.
